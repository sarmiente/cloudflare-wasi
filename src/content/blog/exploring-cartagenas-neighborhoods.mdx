<h1><strong>Exploring Cartagena's Neighborhoods</strong></h1>
Welcome to Cartagena, where history meets modernity, and each neighborhood tells a unique tale of culture, architecture, and vibrant life. Beyond the iconic city walls, Cartagena's neighborhoods beckon with a rich tapestry of experiences. Let's embark on a journey through the captivating streets of Getsemaní, the artistic haven of San Diego, the modern hub of Bocagrande, and more.
